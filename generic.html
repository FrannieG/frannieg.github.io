Frozen Chocolate Banana Recipe:

Makes 4 servings

Ingredients

- 2 ripe but firm bananas - 170 grams chocolate,chopped or semisweet chocolate chips - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil - 1/2cup granola, chopped pecans and walnut and sprinkles (Optional)

Directions

1. Line a baking sheet with nonstick foil or parchment paper. 2. Cut the bananas in half and insert a Popsicle stick into each half, as shown. Place them on the baking sheet and freeze for 15 minutes. 3. Meanwhile, melt the chocolate with the oil in a Pyrex measuring cup in the microwave (check it every 30 seconds) or in a half-full pan of simmering water (about 2 minutes). Stir until smooth. 4. Roll each banana half in the chocolate, then quickly sprinkle with your topping (if using). 5. Freeze until the chocolate sets, 30 minutes. Serve or freeze in an airtight container for up to a week.

Milk Chocolate Recipe:

Serves 1 130g

Ingredients

- 50 grams cocoa butter

- 50grams icing sugar - 25 grams milk powder (Full fat) - 13 grams cocoa powder (Unsweetened) - Steel saucepan and metal utensils

Directions

1. Sieve the icing sugar and cocoa powder together to remove any lumps. Set aside. 2. Blitz the powdered milk in a blender until it is a fine powder. Set aside. 3. Melt the cocoa butter slowly in the saucepan until it is fully melted. 4. Add the milk powder first and stir continually until it is fully dissolved. 5. Add the icing sugar and cocoa powder mixture and stir continuously. 6. Keep stirring until all the ingredients have dissolved and combined to make a smooth, glossy mixture. 7. Remove from the heat for about five minutes, give a final stir and then pour into moulds. 8. Allow to set and enjoy!

Dark Chocolate Repice:

Ingredients

-1/2 cup coconut oil - 1/2 cup cocoa powder - 3 tablespoon honey - 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

Gently melt coconut oil in a saucepan over medium-low heat. Stir cocoa powder, honey, and vanilla extract into melted oil until well blended. Pour mixture into a candy mold or pliable tray. Refrigerate until chilled, about 1 hour.

Caramel Chocolate Recipe:

Ingredients

Caramel: - 1 cup butter - 2 1/4 cups brown sugar - 1 cup light corn syrup - 1 can (14 Ounce) sweetened condensed milk - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract Chocolate: - 1 pound milk chocolate 1 tablespoon butter

Directions

1. Grease an 8 x 8 inch square pan. 2. In a heavy 4 quart saucepan melt butter over medium heat; add brown sugar, corn syrup and milk. Stirring constantly, heat to 242 to 248 degrees F (116 to 120 degrees C), or until a small amount of syrup dropped into cold water forms a firm but pliable ball. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract. Pour into prepared pan. 3. When caramel has cooled and set, cut into 1 inch squares. Chill in refrigerator until firm. 4. Melt chocolate with 1 tablespoon butter in the top of a double boiler or in a bowl in the microwave. Stir until smooth. 5. Dip caramel squares in chocolate and place on wax paper to cool.
